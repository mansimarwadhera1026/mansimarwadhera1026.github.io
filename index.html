About me

I'm a multidisciplinary digital designer based in Vancouver. Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, majoring in Designing Interactions with a minor in Publishing.

My journey began with a foundation in art and illustration and has evolved into a strong focus on design, media and user experience.

I'm particularly interested in user experience, visual design and art direction, exploring how form and function come together to shape meaningful digital experiences.
